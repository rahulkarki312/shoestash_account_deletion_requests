Account Deletion Request for ShoeStash

If you would like to delete your account and associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to rahulkarki.dev@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
  2. In the body of the email, include the email address or username associated with your account.
  3. We will process your request within 30 business days and notify you once your account and data have been deleted.

Data Deletion Information

All personal information such as your name, email address, and preferences will be deleted. Some anonymized data may be retained for 30 days for legal compliance or analytics purposes.
